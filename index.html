I wanted to start by giving a brief description of my work here in Los Angeles. In 2012 I took over management of the Lyric Theatre, a 300 capacity space on La Brea. I transformed the space into a music and comedy venue that quickly became the most sought after venue of its size. I have worked with Golden Voice, Live Nation, ASCAP, NIVA, and our roster included industry giants such as Quincy Jones, Allen Toussaint, and Sarah Silverman. We were most noted for carving a pathway for young players in the industry such as Anderson Paak.
